Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • Heavy cream
  • Whole milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Mix: Combine cream, sugar, milk, and vanilla.
  2. Chill: Refrigerate 2-4 hours or overnight.
  3. Churn: Use ice cream maker, churn 20-30 mins.
  4. Freeze: Transfer to container, freeze 4-6 hours.
  5. Scoop! Enjoy homemade ice cream!
