Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional seasonings (such as oregano, basil, garlic powder)

Steps:

  1. Preheat: Heat oven to 450°F (232°C).
  2. Dough: Roll out dough on cornmeal-dusted sheet.
  3. Sauce: Spread sauce, leaving edges.
  4. Cheese: Layer mozzarella over sauce.
  5. Toppings: Add chosen toppings evenly.
  6. Oil and Season: Drizzle oil, sprinkle seasoning.
  7. Bake: Slide into oven, bake 12-15 mins till golden.
  8. Slice and Enjoy! Cool slightly, slice, and savor!
