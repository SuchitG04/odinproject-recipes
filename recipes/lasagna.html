Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion,garlic and tamato sauce
  • Ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan cheese
  • Egg, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Meat Sauce: Sauté onion, garlic. Brown meat, add tomato sauce, season, simmer.
  2. Noodles: Boil, drain, rinse noodles.
  3. Cheese Filling: Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, pepper.
  4. Assemble: Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat.
  5. Bake: Cover, bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 mins.
  6. Serve: Rest 10-15 mins. Slice and enjoy!
